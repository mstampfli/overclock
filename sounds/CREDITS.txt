Third-party audio bundled with OVERCLOCK and their licenses.

rifle.wav     - single shot from a Carl Gustav M45 (SMG)
deagle.wav    - single shot from a Colt 1911 (.45 pistol)
launcher.wav  - single shot from a Tikka rifle
  All three trimmed + normalized from "The Free Firearm Sound Library"
  (a.k.a. Prepared SFX Library).
  Source : https://opengameart.org/content/the-free-firearm-sound-library
  License: CC0 1.0 (public domain, no rights reserved)

explosion.wav
  Trimmed + normalized from "DeathFlash" (a lossless big-explosion recording).
  Source : https://opengameart.org/content/big-explosion
  License: Creative Commons Attribution 3.0 (CC BY 3.0)

reload.wav
  Real assault-rifle reload (mag out / mag in / charging rack), one channel +
  normalized.
  Source : https://opengameart.org/content/gun-reload-sounds
  License: CC0 1.0 (public domain, no rights reserved)

music_synthwave.wav
  Looping dark-synthwave background bed. Decoded from the OGG, downmixed to one
  channel, high-passed + loudness-normalized (the loop seam is left intact).
  Source : https://opengameart.org/content/synthwave-house-loop
  License: CC0 1.0 (public domain, no rights reserved)

step0.wav .. step5.wav
  Stone/concrete footsteps (3 left + 3 right), from Fantozzi's Footsteps. The
  FLAC stone set, downmixed to one channel, silence-trimmed, high-passed +
  normalized. Laid out [L1,L2,L3,R1,R2,R3] so the game alternates feet.
  Source : https://opengameart.org/content/fantozzis-footsteps-grasssand-stone
  License: CC0 1.0 (public domain, no rights reserved)

hit.wav / headshot.wav / kill.wav
  Combat feedback: hitmarker tick, headshot ding, kill confirm. From Kenney's
  "Interface Sounds" (tick / glass / confirmation), downmixed to one channel,
  high-passed + normalized.
  Source : https://opengameart.org/content/interface-sounds  (kenney.nl)
  License: CC0 1.0 (public domain, no rights reserved)
