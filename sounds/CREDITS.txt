Third-party audio bundled with OVERCLOCK and their licenses.

rifle.wav     - single shot from a Carl Gustav M45 (SMG)
deagle.wav    - single shot from a Colt 1911 (.45 pistol)
launcher.wav  - single shot from a Tikka rifle
  All three trimmed + normalized from "The Free Firearm Sound Library"
  (a.k.a. Prepared SFX Library).
  Source : https://opengameart.org/content/the-free-firearm-sound-library
  License: CC0 1.0 (public domain, no rights reserved)

explosion.wav
  Trimmed + normalized from "DeathFlash" (a lossless big-explosion recording).
  Source : https://opengameart.org/content/big-explosion
  License: Creative Commons Attribution 3.0 (CC BY 3.0)

reload.wav / reload_pistol.wav / reload_launcher.wav
  Per-weapon reloads: assault-rifle reload (rifle), handgun reload (deagle), and
  a heavy mechanical cock (launcher). One channel, silence-trimmed + normalized.
  Source : https://opengameart.org/content/gun-reload-sounds
  License: CC0 1.0 (public domain, no rights reserved)

music_synthwave.wav
  Looping dark-synthwave background bed. Decoded from the OGG, downmixed to one
  channel, high-passed, presence/treble-lifted (it read muffled in mono) +
  loudness-normalized (the loop seam is left intact).
  Source : https://opengameart.org/content/synthwave-house-loop
  License: CC0 1.0 (public domain, no rights reserved)

step0.wav .. step5.wav
  Stone/concrete footsteps (3 left + 3 right), from Fantozzi's Footsteps. The
  FLAC stone set, downmixed to one channel, silence-trimmed, high-passed +
  normalized. Laid out [L1,L2,L3,R1,R2,R3] so the game alternates feet.
  Source : https://opengameart.org/content/fantozzis-footsteps-grasssand-stone
  License: CC0 1.0 (public domain, no rights reserved)

hit.wav / headshot.wav / kill.wav
  Combat feedback: hitmarker tick, headshot ding, kill confirm. From Kenney's
  "Interface Sounds" (tick / glass / confirmation), downmixed to one channel,
  high-passed + normalized.
  Source : https://opengameart.org/content/interface-sounds  (kenney.nl)
  License: CC0 1.0 (public domain, no rights reserved)

pickup_health.wav / pickup_shield.wav / pickup_ammo.wav
  Pickup chimes (health / shield cell / ammo). From Kenney's "Interface Sounds"
  (pluck / bong / drop), downmixed to one channel, high-passed + normalized.
  Source : https://opengameart.org/content/interface-sounds  (kenney.nl)
  License: CC0 1.0 (public domain, no rights reserved)

overclock.wav
  The overclock activation surge. The "teleport" sound from a CC0 sci-fi SFX
  set, downmixed to one channel + normalized.
  Source : https://opengameart.org/content/50-cc0-sci-fi-sfx
  License: CC0 1.0 (public domain, no rights reserved)

ambience.wav
  Looping arena ambience bed (low sci-fi room tone). The "loop_ambient_01" loop
  from the same CC0 sci-fi set, downmixed to one channel + normalized; the loop
  seam is left intact.
  Source : https://opengameart.org/content/50-cc0-sci-fi-sfx
  License: CC0 1.0 (public domain, no rights reserved)

swing.wav / slash.wav / throw.wav
  Katana swoosh, slash connect and grenade-throw whoosh, from the "Swishes Sound
  Pack" (swish air sounds), downmixed to one channel, silence-trimmed + normalized.
  Source : https://opengameart.org/content/swishes-sound-pack
  License: CC0 1.0 (public domain, no rights reserved)

grapple.wav
  Grapple-fire launch. The "shoot_01" sound from the CC0 sci-fi set, downmixed
  to one channel, trimmed + normalized.
  Source : https://opengameart.org/content/50-cc0-sci-fi-sfx
  License: CC0 1.0 (public domain, no rights reserved)

hurt.wav / death.wav
  Taking damage + dying: body-hit impacts from "37 hits/punches". Downmixed to
  one channel, trimmed (tail shortened) + normalized; hurt is a short punch,
  death keeps more of the impact tail.
  Source : https://opengameart.org/content/37-hitspunches
  License: CC0 1.0 (public domain, no rights reserved)
